What's New

  • THE AAJIVAN LECTURE SERIES

    This is a newly established lecture series that will run annually beginning in August 2019 with lectures being held once a month. The lectures are open to students in Grades 9, 10, 11 and 12 and will take place during the school day, lasting an hour including a question and answer session. Lectures will be given by a wide range of thought leaders, including alumni and parents, covering a variety of topics to further enhance and educate our students in the world outside of TISB.

  • Creative Arts

    There are many opportunities for students to excel is Art, Drama and Music throughout their time at TISB, whether as a hobby taking part in one of the many clubs on offer, or more seriously at IGCSE and IB levels.
